How It Works

Providing Liquidity

01

Connect Wallet

Connect your wallet on the Base network. You'll need both tokens in the pair to provide liquidity.

02

Choose a Pool

Select FIGI/ETH, FIGI/USDC, or FIGI/BTC. Each pool earns 0.3% on every swap through it.

03

Deposit Tokens

Approve and deposit an equal value of both tokens. Your position is minted as an LP token.

04

Earn Fees

Every trade through your pool earns you a proportional share of the 0.3% fee. Withdraw anytime.
