Who We Are

Bridging the Physical and Digital

Figital is a real-world asset integration company. We purchase, authenticate, and vault physical luxury goods — then tokenize them on the blockchain so they can be owned, traded, and verified by anyone, anywhere.

◆

Self-Custodied Vault

Every listed asset is physically held in our secure vault. We are the asset holder — no third-party custody risk.

☍

On-Chain Ownership

The Process

How It Works

01

We Acquire & Authenticate

Figital purchases physical assets — jewelry, silver, watches — and verifies their authenticity and condition.

02

Vault & Document

Assets are stored in our secure vault. Each item is photographed, cataloged, and assigned a unique identifier.

03

Tokenize On-Chain

A digital token is minted representing the physical asset. The token is backed 1:1 by the vaulted item.

04

Trade & Redeem

Token holders can trade their assets on-chain or redeem the token for physical delivery of the item.
